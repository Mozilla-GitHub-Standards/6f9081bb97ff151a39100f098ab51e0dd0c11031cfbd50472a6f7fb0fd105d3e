About Synbiota

Synbiota radically reduces barrier-to-entry for creating blockbuster Life Science, Health, Cleantech, and Greentech products by virtualizing the entire Synthetic Biology research and development pipeline. With Synbiota, anyone with a web-connected device, and a desire to change the world can leverage Biotechnology to create disruptive applications and products in medicine, materials, food and fuel. Using Synbiota, groups of any size, or even an individual can, with the click of a button, create a virtual open research institute complete with DNA development tools, integrated data storage and management, colleagues, secure communication, attribution, IP, and performance metrics.
