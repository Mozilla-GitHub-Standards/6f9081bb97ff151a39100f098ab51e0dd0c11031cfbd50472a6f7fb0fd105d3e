Entropy Wave

About Entropy Wave

Entropy Wave provides complete systems for video encoding and distribution to content producers and distributors, as well as a Gstreamer-based software media platform for application and embedded developers. We offer a range of solutions which enable customers to benefit from the freedom and cost-effectiveness of open source and open video technology.
