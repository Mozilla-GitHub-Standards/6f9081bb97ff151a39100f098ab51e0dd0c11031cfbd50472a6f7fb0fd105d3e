About

I've been programming in the free and open source community ever since I stumbled upon the GNU Manifesto in high school. My past work has been primarily in the Python community, contributing projects like WebOb, pip, virtualenv, and Paste. In my time at Mozilla I've moved into the world Javascript, and am hoping to increasingly pick up my interest in education that I've only been able to pursue lightly in the past. I work from home in Minneapolis.
